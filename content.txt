Arsinoitherium
Arsinoitherium is an extinct genus of paenungulate mammal belonging to the extinct order Embrithopoda; related to elephants, sirenians, hyraxes and the extinct desmostylians.

Glyptodon
Glyptodon was a genus of large, armored mammals of the subfamily Glyptodontinae—relatives of armadillos—that lived during the Pleistocene epoch.

Elasmotherium
Elasmotherium, also known as the Siberian Unicorn, is an extinct genus of rhinoceros endemic to Eurasia during the Late Pliocene through the Pleistocene.
